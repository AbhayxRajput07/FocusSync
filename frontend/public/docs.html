GETTING STARTED

Welcome to FocusSync

FocusSync is not another productivity timer. It is a precision-engineered focus enforcement system built for people who are serious about deep work.

💡

What makes FocusSync different?

Most focus apps rely on your willpower. FocusSync removes willpower from the equation entirely. Once a session begins — there is no exit. Only execution.

Focus Shield

Module: MOD_01 Type: KERNEL LEVEL ACTIVE
Focus Shield is the enforcement layer of FocusSync. It monitors all browser activity and escalates interventions based on behavioral violations.

Intervention Levels

⚠️

Gentle Warning

A subtle overlay appears reminding you to refocus. Score penalty: -2pts

🔴

Screen Interruption

Full screen overlay blocks content. Must acknowledge to continue. Score penalty: -5pts

🔒

Hard Screen Lock

Complete session lock. Cannot proceed until timer expires. Score penalty: -15pts
